# Madhav E-Commerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, and payment preferences for an e-commerce business. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and identify areas for business growth.

## Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of transactional data. This project aims to transform raw sales data into actionable insights by providing a centralized dashboard that helps management track sales trends, customer performance, and product profitability.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Data Modeling
* Microsoft Excel

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales Amount: 438K
* Total Profit: 37K
* Total Quantity Sold: 6K
* Average Order Value (AOV): 121K

## Dashboard Features

### Sales Analysis

* Sales performance by state
* Customer-wise revenue analysis
* Quarterly sales filtering

### Profit Analysis

* Monthly profit trends
* Profit by product sub-category

### Customer & Product Insights

* Top contributing customers
* Product category performance
* Quantity sold by category

### Payment Analysis

* Distribution of orders by payment mode
* Customer payment preferences

## Key Insights

* Maharashtra generated the highest sales revenue among all states.
* Printers and Bookcases were the most profitable product sub-categories.
* Profitability fluctuated significantly throughout the year, with losses observed during several months.
* A large proportion of orders were completed through a single dominant payment method, accounting for approximately 44% of total transactions.
* A small group of customers contributed a significant share of total revenue, indicating opportunities for customer retention strategies.

## Business Recommendations

* Investigate causes of profit decline during loss-making months.
* Increase marketing efforts in high-performing states to maximize revenue.
* Focus inventory and promotional campaigns on high-profit product categories.
* Develop loyalty programs for top customers to improve retention and repeat purchases.
* Encourage adoption of preferred payment methods to improve customer experience.

## Project Outcome

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales and profitability, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions regarding product strategy, customer engagement, and regional sales performance.
